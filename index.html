About

Kaz n' Kazoo is a collection of various design interests focused on the TRADITIONAL x MODERN of Japanese design. It showcases the beauty and craftsmanship of traditional design and how it has evolved for the modern day world. I hope you enjoy reading and learning about Japanese design as much as I do.
